Bulk send

Send one PDF to many people at once — each recipient gets their own independent document, signing link, audit trail, and sealed final PDF.

1 · Choose a draft document

Only drafts are listed. Upload a PDF from the dashboard first if you don't see yours.

2 · Choose a single-signer template

Templates are saved from the prepare editor. Only templates whose fields all belong to signer 1 can be used for bulk send.

3 · Recipients

Formats: Name, email or Name <email> — up to 100 per batch.
